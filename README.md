```
'''
Given a rock with mass $m_{rock}$ thrown by a force of $F_0$ acting over a duration $dt_{force}$,
calculate its airtime and the max height it achieves.
'''

''' Given: '''
g = 9.81 m/s^2
m_{rock} = 10 kg
F_0 = 1000 N
dt_{force} = 0.1 s


''' Solution: '''
y_{vel} = F_0 / m_{rock} * dt_{force} = ?

airtime = y_{vel} / g * 2 = ?
height_{max} = y_{vel} * airtime / 4 = ? cm
```
